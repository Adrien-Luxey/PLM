Caça à lesma

Now that your isFacingTrail() method is working, it's time to write the code to organize the hunting party. Copy/paste your code from the previous exercise, and complete the hunt() method.

Following a track is not very difficult: move forward as long as you have the track in front of you. If there is not track in front of you anymore, check whether the rest of the track is on your left or on your right, and follow it further.

Para garantir que você não misture o caminho de onde você veio com o para onde você vai, a forma mais fácil é apagar o caminho por onde você passa. Use o método abaixarPincel() para abaixar seu pincel e marcar o chão, e levantarPincel() para levantá-lo de novo.

Finally, do not forget to capture your prey once you found it (using pickupBaggle()).

Objetivo do exercício

Complete the hunt() method. You probably want to copy over the isFacingTrail() method that you wrote in previous exercise.
